MASTER SERVICES AGREEMENT

This Master Services Agreement (“Agreement”) is made and entered into on this 1st day of January 2024 (“Effective Date”), by and between ABC Technologies Private Limited, a company incorporated under the Companies Act, 2013, having its registered office at Pune, Maharashtra (hereinafter referred to as the “Client”), and XYZ Digital Solutions Private Limited, having its principal office at Bengaluru, Karnataka (hereinafter referred to as the “Service Provider”).

1. DEFINITIONS
For the purposes of this Agreement, the following terms shall have the meanings assigned to them:
1.1 “Services” means software development, implementation, customization, integration, maintenance, support, and consulting services described in Statements of Work.
1.2 “Deliverables” means all documents, reports, code, software modules, configurations, and materials produced under this Agreement.
1.3 “Confidential Information” means any proprietary information disclosed by one party to the other.
1.4 “Intellectual Property Rights” means copyrights, patents, trademarks, trade secrets, and other proprietary rights.

2. SCOPE OF SERVICES
The Service Provider shall provide Services as described in individual Statements of Work (“SOW”) executed by the parties. Each SOW shall define project scope, timelines, milestones, fees, and acceptance criteria.

The Service Provider shall allocate qualified personnel with appropriate skills and experience. The Client may reasonably request replacement of personnel for performance or security concerns.

3. CHANGE MANAGEMENT
Any modification to the scope of Services shall require a written Change Order signed by both parties. The Change Order shall specify impact on timeline, deliverables, and fees.

4. TERM AND TERMINATION
This Agreement shall commence on the Effective Date and continue for three years unless terminated earlier.
Either party may terminate for convenience with ninety (90) days’ written notice.
Either party may terminate immediately upon material breach if such breach remains uncured for thirty (30) days after notice.

5. FEES AND PAYMENT TERMS
The Client shall pay fees as set forth in the applicable SOW.
Invoices shall be payable within thirty (30) days of receipt.
Late payments may attract interest at 12% per annum.
Taxes, duties, and levies shall be borne by the Client except income taxes of the Service Provider.

6. ACCEPTANCE AND TESTING
Deliverables shall be subject to acceptance testing.
If Deliverables fail to meet agreed specifications, the Service Provider shall rectify defects within a reasonable period.

7. WARRANTIES
The Service Provider warrants that:
(a) Services shall be performed in a professional manner;
(b) Deliverables shall substantially conform to specifications;
(c) Services will not infringe third-party intellectual property rights.

8. CONFIDENTIALITY
Each party shall protect Confidential Information using reasonable care.
Confidential Information shall not be disclosed to third parties without prior written consent except as required by law.
Obligations of confidentiality shall survive termination for five (5) years.

9. DATA PROTECTION
The Service Provider shall comply with applicable data protection laws including the Information Technology Act, 2000.
The Service Provider shall implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures.
In the event of a data breach, the Service Provider shall notify the Client within 48 hours.

10. INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY
All Deliverables created specifically for the Client shall be the exclusive property of the Client upon full payment.
Pre-existing intellectual property shall remain the property of the owning party.

11. INDEMNIFICATION
The Service Provider shall indemnify and hold harmless the Client from claims arising from:
(a) Intellectual property infringement;
(b) Gross negligence;
(c) Breach of confidentiality.

12. LIMITATION OF LIABILITY
Except for indemnification and confidentiality obligations, neither party shall be liable for indirect, incidental, or consequential damages.
Total liability shall not exceed the total fees paid under the applicable SOW.

13. NON-SOLICITATION
Neither party shall solicit employees of the other party during the term of this Agreement and for one year thereafter.

14. FORCE MAJEURE
Neither party shall be liable for delay caused by events beyond reasonable control including natural disasters, war, or government actions.

15. DISPUTE RESOLUTION
Disputes shall be resolved through arbitration in accordance with the Arbitration and Conciliation Act, 1996.
The seat of arbitration shall be Mumbai.

16. GOVERNING LAW
This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of India.

17. ASSIGNMENT
Neither party may assign this Agreement without prior written consent except in connection with a merger or acquisition.

18. ENTIRE AGREEMENT
This Agreement constitutes the entire agreement between the parties and supersedes prior discussions.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, the parties have executed this Agreement as of the Effective Date.
