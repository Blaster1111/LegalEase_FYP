1.  DEFINITIONS AND INTERPRETATION 1.1 “Affiliate” means any entity or

3.  DUTIES AND OBLIGATIONS 3.1 The Employee shall perform duties

-   Follow company rules and regulations.
-   Devote full working time to the Company.
-   Act in the best interests of the Company.
-   Avoid conflicts of interest.
-   Maintain professional conduct.

3.3 The Employee shall not: - Engage in competing business during
employment or for one year after termination. - Disclose Confidential
Information. - Solicit company clients, employees, or vendors.

    employment shall belong to the Company. The Employee shall assist in
    securing such rights.

7.  ENTIRE AGREEMENT This Agreement supersedes all prior understandings
    and may only be amended in writing.

8.  REPRESENTATIONS The Employee confirms that no prior agreements
    restrict employment with the Company.

9.  TERMINATION 9.1 The Company may terminate employment with four
    weeks’ notice. 9.2 The Employee may resign with twelve weeks’
    notice. 9.3 Immediate termination may occur for misconduct, fraud,
    or serious breach. 9.4 Upon termination, the Employee shall:

-   Return company property.
-   Not represent association with the Company.
-   Maintain confidentiality.

10. CONFIDENTIALITY The Employee shall not disclose or use Confidential
    Information during or after employment without authorization.

11. INDEMNITY The Employee shall indemnify the Company against losses
    arising from breaches of representations.

12. DISPUTE RESOLUTION Disputes shall be resolved through arbitration in
    accordance with the Arbitration and Conciliation Act, 1996. Venue:
